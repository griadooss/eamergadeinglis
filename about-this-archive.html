About this archive

This website is a permanent, unchanging copy of the Eamer | Gade | Inglis family history. It was preserved as a fixed set of pages so the family’s story remains freely available for generations to come — independent of any subscription, hosting service, or software that might one day disappear.

When the research dates from

The genealogy was compiled chiefly between 2007 and 2009, with occasional additions over the following decade and a last period of activity around 2019–2021. It was captured in this fixed form in 2026 and is no longer being added to or corrected.

About “living” people

When the research was gathered, some people recorded here were still living. That status reflects information which is now, in most cases, fifteen to twenty years old. Out of respect for privacy, individuals who were living at that time are not listed in the site’s search and are kept out of search-engine indexes, though their pages remain part of the record.

In time, of course, everyone named here becomes part of history. Wherever this site distinguishes “living” from “deceased”, and wherever an age or a “living” note appears, it should be read as a statement about 2007–2009 — not about the present day.

Custodian

This archive is preserved and cared for by John G Eamer. The complete underlying research is held privately by the custodian.

If you share ancestry with these families, or have a correction or a question, you are warmly welcome to write to contact@eamergadeinglis.net.
